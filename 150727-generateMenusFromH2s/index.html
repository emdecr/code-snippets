About Me

HELLO! I’m a Front-End Web Developer based in Toronto, Canada who is currently studying at HackerYou. Through design and code I aim to create beautiful sites through which people can intuitively – and enjoyably – navigate.

My academic background, rooted in psychology and English, only fostered my passion towards mental health and words (in whatever format). When designing and developing sites, I try to focus on incorporating aspects from cognitive and narrative frameworks in order to offer your audience the optimal browsing experience.

I would love to help you share your story in a visually engaging way on the web, so you can make more connections in your daily life.
